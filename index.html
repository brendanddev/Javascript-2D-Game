DodgeBall Game

Assignment 4 - SVG and JavaScript

How to play:

  • Move the ball around with the 'w', 'a', 's', 'd' keys on the keyboard
  • Or, you can move the ball around with the use of the mouse
  • Choose which option at the 'Control Options' part of the menu.
  • To reset the movement from Mouse, to Keyboard, refresh the page
  • Avoid the spikes to stay alive, every second alive counts as +1 score
  • Make contact with blue and red balls to get +10 points
    • When you make contact with a red and blue item, you will grow in size.
  • Make contact with a green and purple ball to get +5 points
    • When you make contact with a green and pruple ball, you will shrink in size.
    • This can only be done when you have grown atleast one time.
  • How long can you stay alive?!
